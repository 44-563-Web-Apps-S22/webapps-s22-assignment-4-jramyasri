Tartuffe

ACT 2

SCENE I

Orgon, Marine and her servant-- Orgon, Mariane, Emlire
ELMIRE
This is a perfect place for such a purpose. There now, it's okay. Mariane, I find You endowed with a heart that's sweet and kind And you have always been most dear to me.

MARIANE
[He begins to leave.]

A father's love brings true felicity.

ORGON

Well said, my child! And to earn it fully You should devote yourself to contenting me. Mariane. That's how my devotion is put to the proof. Orgon. Good. Now what do you think of our guest, Tartuffe?

MARIANE

Think well before you reply. Oh my! Tell me what to say . . . and I'll comply.
